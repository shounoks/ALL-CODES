Shiv :
Shiv is often called "The Destroyer," Shiva represents the transformative power of the universe. He is the supreme ascetic, typically depicted in deep meditation on Mount Kailash. His role is to destroy ego and ignorance, clearing the path for new creation.
Kali :
Kali is fierce manifestation of the Divine Mother, Kali is the goddess of time, change, and empowerment. She represents the raw, untamable energy of nature. While her appearance can be intimidating—often depicted with a garland of skulls—she is deeply revered as a protector who destroys evil to protect the innocent.
