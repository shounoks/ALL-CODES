Shiv Ratri

Shiv Ratri is a very special day in a Shiva devotee's life.
In this night there will be four part known as Parahar. In this day many devotee offers milk,sweets,ghee,butter,flower,candle etc to Lord Shiva. Mahadev is also known as Chandrashekhara.
Beacuse he saved Chandra dev from Prajapati Daksha's curse. Let's Say Together :

Har Har Mahadev
